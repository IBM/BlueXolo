Login Request Access

If you already have an account login to BlueXolo.
If you do not have an account or if you have forgotten your password, continue with the guide to learn how to request access.

Request Access

To request access to the tool, click the “Request Access” button.

Request Access

Once you have accessed this menu, introduce your email, select a product, and click on “SEND REQUEST”.
When your administrator has granted your access, you will receive an email of authorization.

Request Access

Once you have received the “Access Authorization” by your admin follow the instructions, assign your password, and enjoy the tool!

Forgotten Password

To request a new password, click on the “I forgot my password” button.

Forgotten Password

Once you have received the email with the instructions, click on the link that come in the email follow the instructions, assign your password, and enjoy the tool!

Creating Parameters

Once you have displayed the menu look for the servers' section and click on the “Parameter” section.

Add a New Parameter Template

Once you have accessed go to the right section at bottom of the page and look for the button with the character “+” and click on it to create a new parameter template.

Creating a New Parameter

In order to create a new "Parameter" you need to select a Category Group, assign name, a brief description of the parameter and click the save button.
You can add as many parameters as needed for your execution.
Take into consideration that the parameters need to be assigned to a “Category Group” and they can only be used under that category.
